# 🖼️ Character card

The Character card shows what your AI character looks like. It goes well with the character's name and other details to make your character stand out and be more interesting.

- Character card is mandatory. You won't be able to create a character without a character card.
- Character card can be in various formats including PNG, GIF, and JPEG. We
  recommend using a portrait aspect ratio image that is 1024x1792 for best
  display results.

Pick or make a character card that fits your AI character. A good character card makes interacting with your character better.

## Generate character card

You can generate an character card using our images feature. Simply click on 'Generate', and enter a prompt describing the kind of image you want to create.
