# Welcome to Openroleplay.ai!

At Open Roleplay, we're dedicated to democratizing AI companionship, offering a platform for creating AI characters and engaging in roleplaying adventures with complete control over your data, models, and characters. Our aim is to craft a space where interactions with AI friends and the exploration of fantasies become routine, all powered by cutting-edge AI technology.

Bringing this dream to life is a team effort - we need you on board! Growing from our cozy community to a full-blown platform is no small feat. It means beefing up our infrastructure, growing our team, and keeping everything running smoothly. We're thinking about adding some paid options down the line to help with this, but don't worry - we're dead set on keeping the main features free for everyone.
